Boba

Boba is also known as Tapioca balls and are small "pearls" of tapioca which are cooked before being added to drinks. Currently we have two kinds of "mini" tapioca pearls. One bag is brown and one bag is white. After some investigation, I have determined our problem. We just have small tapioca pearls, not "mini boba"!. Additionally, the white tapioca pearls will almost definitely not work the way we think it will. This is because black colored boba is in fact black because of the added sugar. It is possible that we can achieve this result by cooking the pearls with brown sugar. However, the largest problem is that we selected tapioca pearls used in dessert cooking and pudding!

So, we need to get large tapioca pearls.

Tea

We have green tea. The tea we made was fine, but we should probably brew a much stronger tea next time.

Milk

Last time we used 2% milk and blended it together with the tea and ice. We could use sweetened condensed milk next time to make the drink thicker and sweeter.

Sweetener

Last time we used brown sugar. I would imagine this is actually fine. We just need more than expected.
